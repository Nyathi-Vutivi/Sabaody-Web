Our Story

Back in 2025, Vutivi and Masebola (that's us!) started Sabaody with one simple idea: websites should be beautiful AND actually work for your business. We were tired of seeing businesses get stuck with expensive, ineffective sites.

Why "Sabaody"? It's from a legend about a paradise where people stay forever young. That's our goal—creating websites that feel fresh and relevant for years to come.
